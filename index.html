About US

Asoka World School

Asoka World School is one of the pioneers in Kerala to integrate Google for Education in classrooms. The global platform creates a collaborative environment for teachers and students to leverage technology intelligently. Video conferences enable classes while customized web applications are used to grade the performance of students and keep parents in the loop. At the best school in Kochi, all students are provided with their school email accounts with full access to Google Apps for Education.
